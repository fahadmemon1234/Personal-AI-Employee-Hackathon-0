# LinkedIn Poster Skill

This skill generates and posts content to LinkedIn to drive business growth and sales.

## Purpose
- Generate engaging LinkedIn content based on business goals
- Schedule and post updates to LinkedIn
- Implement approval workflow for all posts
- Track and report on LinkedIn engagement

## Functionality
- Analyzes business goals and recent activities to generate relevant content
- Creates draft posts in Pending_Approval directory for review
- Posts approved content to LinkedIn (simulated in current implementation)
- Logs all LinkedIn activity in Dashboard.md
- Archives completed posts for record keeping

## Configuration
Requires LinkedIn API credentials (not included in current implementation due to API limitations)

## Usage
This skill is automatically invoked by the LinkedIn poster system when content generation or posting is needed.
